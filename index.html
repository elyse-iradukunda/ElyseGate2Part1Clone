Our rankings and reputation

World Top 150

QS World University Rankings 2026 places us in the Top 150 global universities. We've moved up to 147th in the world, climbing 43 places in the last 3 years and up from 165th in the 2025 rankings.

Teaching Gold Our teaching is rated Gold, the highest level, by the Teaching Excellence Framework.

We're ranked 19th overall in the United Kingdom in the Complete University Guide's UK League Table.

We’re ranked in the top 160 universities globally in the Times Higher Education World University rankings.
